Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I place an order?

Browse our collections, select the items you love, choose your size, and click "Add to Cart". When you're ready, go to your cart and follow the checkout process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including M-Pesa, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and other mobile money options for your convenience.

How long does delivery take?

Deliveries within Nairobi typically take 1-2 business days. For the rest of Kenya, please allow 2-4 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order is shipped.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items that are in their original, unworn condition with all tags attached. Please visit our Terms & Conditions page for more details on our return process.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email and/or SMS with a tracking link that you can use to monitor your delivery's progress.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently, we are an online-only store. This allows us to offer you the best prices on the latest Kenyan fashion. Our main office is located in Bungoma, Kenya.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at info@borefashion.com or on WhatsApp at +254714663058. We're happy to help!
